Rules:
  • Includes mostly masculine (-us, -er, -ir) and neuter (-um) nouns.
  • Neuter Rule: The nominative and accusative (and vocative) cases are identical, and in the plural they end in -a.
  • The vocative of masculine nouns ending in -us ends in -e (e.g., serve). Vocative of nouns ending in -ius ends in -ī (e.g., fīlī).
